History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate innovators. Over the years, it has grown into a leading global technology conference, showcasing breakthroughs in AI, robotics, and sustainable computing.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire innovation, support diverse voices in technology, and build a collaborative future. TechCon serves as a hub where creativity meets technology, connecting people with ideas and opportunities.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted inspiring leaders who’ve shaped industries and changed lives:

  • Dr. Alice Carter – AI pioneer who advanced ethical machine learning.

  • Mark Liu – Semiconductor leader recognized for global supply chain innovations.

  • Sophia Reyes – Entrepreneur dedicated to bringing tech education to underserved communities.
